Privacy Statement

Last updated: April 2026

Your personal data is treated with the same care as your Blueprint. We collect only what is necessary, use it only for what we promise, and never share it without your explicit consent.

02

What data we collect and why

When you order your SZINN Alignment Blueprint, we ask for the following personal data:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth, time of birth and place of birth
  • Payment data (processed by Plug&Pay / Mollie — we do not store payment details)

We use this data exclusively to:

  • Calculate your personalised Blueprint based on your exact birth data
  • Deliver your Blueprint via PDF and personal HTML link
  • Send you order confirmation and delivery emails via Enormail
  • Respond to your questions or requests

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling or automated decision-making that produces legal effects.

◆
03

Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under the GDPR:

  • Performance of a contract — to deliver the Blueprint you purchased
  • Legitimate interest — to improve our service and communicate about your order
  • Consent — for any email marketing, which you can withdraw at any time
◆
04

Who we share your data with

We work with a small number of trusted partners to deliver your Blueprint:

  • Plug&Pay — payment processing
  • Mollie — payment processing
  • Tally — intake form for your birth data
  • Make.com — automation of Blueprint delivery
  • Enormail — email delivery
  • Tiiny.host / Netlify — hosting of your personal HTML link

All partners are required to handle your data in accordance with GDPR. We do not sell your data to third parties. Ever.

◆
05

How long we keep your data

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to deliver and support your Blueprint, and as required by Dutch tax law (7 years for financial records). Your birth data and Blueprint are stored for the duration of your access to your personal HTML link.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing info@szinn.ai. We will process your request within 30 days.

◆
06

Your rights under GDPR

As a data subject under European law, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data
  • Right to restriction — you can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a structured format
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to withdraw consent — for any consent-based processing, at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@szinn.ai. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens) at autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl.

07

Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies. We use functional cookies only — to remember your consent choice and ensure the website works correctly. We do not use tracking, advertising or analytical cookies without your explicit consent.

You can withdraw your cookie consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies or contacting us.

08

Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss or misuse. Your personal HTML link is unique and private. Payment data is processed via SSL-encrypted connections and never stored on our servers.
